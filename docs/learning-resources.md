## Certifications (when ready to go further)

Salesforce certifications validate your knowledge and are widely recognized in the industry.

| Certification | Target audience | Level |
|---|---|---|
| [Salesforce Administrator](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/administrator) | Admins, ops | Entry |
| [Platform App Builder](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/platformappbuilder) | Admins who build apps | Entry |
| [Platform Developer I](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/platformdeveloperi) | Developers | Entry |
| [Platform Developer II](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/platformdeveloperii) | Developers | Advanced |
| [JavaScript Developer I](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/javascriptdeveloperi) | JS/LWC developers | Entry |

All certification guides and practice exams are on Trailhead.
